Our work

What is Data Vinci?

Data Vinci helps companies create insightful dashboards. We have worked with major corporations and organizations in the retail, healthcare, consulting, non-profit and energy industries.


Data Vinci's values

When it comes to Tableau, everyone seems to be satisfied with how-tos. People only seem to ask things like:
How do I recreate this table I made in PowerPoint?
How do I vary the width of each bar on a vertical bar chart?
It is easy to learn to do things in Tableau. It is much harder to understand why one should.
Unlike others, we care to know how each design choice could affect the experience of our end users.

So we would rather ask:
Should I recreate this table I made in PowerPoint?
Should I vary the width of each bar in a vertical bar chart?
Everyone seems to be obsessed with Tableau as a tool because it makes everything easier for them.
We are obsessed with what we've made with it because we know we've made everything easier for you.
